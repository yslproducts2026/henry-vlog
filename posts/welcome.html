Welcome to My Vlog

June 10, 2026 · Henry Han

Hey there! Welcome to my personal vlog.

I've been thinking about starting this for a while. Running a furniture manufacturing business keeps me busy, but I've always wanted a space to share what I learn along the way — the wins, the challenges, and everything in between.

At YSL Products, we make chairs, dining sets, sofas, beds, and all kinds of furniture. We ship to over 50 countries. But beyond the numbers and the factory floor, there are stories worth telling.

What to Expect

I'll be writing about:

Furniture Design & Manufacturing — How products go from sketch to container. The materials we choose, the quality checks, the late-night problem solving.

Business & Markets — Insights from working with clients across South America, the Middle East, Europe, and beyond.

Travel — Trip recaps, market visits, and the occasional food recommendation from cities I visit for work.

Life — Random thoughts, lessons learned, and the balance between hustle and family.

I'll keep it real. No fluff. Just honest stories from someone who loves what he does.

Thanks for reading. More coming soon.

— Henry
