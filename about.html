About Me

Hi, I'm Henry Han. I run YSL Products Co., Ltd., a furniture manufacturing company based in China. We specialize in chairs, dining sets, upholstery, beds, sofas, and wood furniture — exported to over 50 countries worldwide.

This vlog is where I share stories from the factory floor, travel experiences, market insights, and the occasional life lesson. Whether it's a new design coming together, a challenging shipment, or a great meal in a new city — you'll find it here.

When I'm not working, I enjoy photography, exploring new places, and spending time with family.

Thanks for stopping by. Feel free to reach out at henry.han@yslproducts.com.
